Company Overview

Neumann Intelligence is an AI-as-a-Service (AIaaS) company based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. The company focuses on building scalable, production-ready artificial intelligence solutions for businesses across multiple domains, including recruitment technology (HRTech) and healthcare technology (HealthTech).
Neumann Intelligence develops AI-powered software products that automate repetitive workflows, improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making through data-driven insights.
The company’s core mission is to make advanced AI technologies accessible to businesses of all sizes without requiring in-house AI expertise.
